Algebra Basics
Negative Numbers
Addition and Subtraction

Use these rules when the signs are next to each other.
+ + makes plus
(E.G. 3 + 6 = 9)
+ - makes minus
(E.G. 3 + -6 = -3)
- + makes minus
(E.G. 3 - 6 = -3)
- - makes plus
(E.G. 3 - -6 = 9)
Multiplication and Division

Always use these rules when multiplying or dividing.
+ + makes plus
(E.G. 6 / 2 = 3)
+ - makes minus
(E.G. 6 / -2 = -3)
- + makes minus
(E.G. -6 / 2 = -3)
- - makes plus
(E.G. -6 / -2 = 3)
