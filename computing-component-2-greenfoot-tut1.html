Greenfoot: How to add an actor

This is the editor. The green part is the worlds of the game. The worlds are where the game is happening (in this game, the world is called 'WombatWorld'). The yellow section is the actors (characters or objects) of the game. The blue part is where you can see the world, the actors, and the events of the game.



To add an actor to the world, right-click (control-click on mac) on the actor name and select "new [actor name]()". In this example, it reads "new Wombat()"



Hover the mouse over the place you would like to put the actor, and click to place it.



To save the actors to the default setting of the world, right-click on the world background and select "Save the world".
